Terms of Use

These terms outline how Sportivo product browsing, checkout, account, and order features are presented to shoppers.

1. Shopping experience

Sportivo lets shoppers browse sports gear, manage a cart, save products, place an order, and follow order status from one consistent interface.

2. No real purchases

Product availability, checkout totals, order status, and delivery dates are presented for the shopping experience. No live payment gateway or fulfillment service is connected in this version.

3. Local storage

Sportivo stores cart items, wishlist items, checkout preferences, and order history on your device so the experience remains available after refreshing the page.

4. Product content

Product names, descriptions, ratings, images, and delivery information are curated for the Sportivo catalog and may not represent official brand information, live pricing, or real-time inventory.

5. Limitations

This version does not include a live payment gateway, production authentication, real inventory management, or carrier fulfillment integration.
