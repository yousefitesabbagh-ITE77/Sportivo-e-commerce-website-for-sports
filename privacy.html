Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy explains how Sportivo handles shopping data on your device.

Contents

  1. Shopping data
  2. Local storage usage
  3. Account session
  4. Third-party services
  5. Clearing your data

1. Shopping data

Sportivo uses the information entered during checkout to build your order summary and delivery view. Payment card details are not collected or processed.

2. Local storage usage

Sportivo saves cart items, wishlist items, account session details, and order history in browser storage. The saved data remains on your device.

3. Account session

The account area validates form fields and keeps your shopping session available on the current device. Passwords are not sent to a live authentication server.

4. Third-party services

Sportivo may load fonts from Google Fonts. Product, cart, wishlist, and order information are handled inside the site experience.

5. Clearing your data

You can clear saved shopping data by clearing this site's browser storage from your browser settings.
