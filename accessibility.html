1. Our Commitment

Sportivo Sports is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone, and applying the relevant accessibility standards to ensure we provide equal access to all our users.

2. Measures to Support Accessibility

We have implemented the following measures to ensure the accessibility of our website:

  • Providing continual accessibility training for our staff.
  • Assigning clear accessibility targets and responsibilities.
  • Employing formal accessibility quality assurance methods.
  • Ensuring our website is navigable via keyboard and screen reader.
  • Using clear and simple language.
  • Providing alt text for all meaningful images.

3. Conformance Status

We aim to adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website and in doing so adhere to many of the available standards and guidelines.
